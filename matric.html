Matric programs

The Study ZONE Academy, we blend knowledge with excellence to create leaders of tomorrow. Our Matric programs lay the foundation for strong academic and personal growth, fostering an environment where creativity and critical thinking thrive.

About Program

Study Zone Academy has introduced the innovative concept of Pre-9th Class, addressing the challenge of limited time for students to cover the 9th class course effectively. This comprehensive program spans over 3-4 months and empowers students to get ahead in their studies and be well-prepared for their matric exams.

About Program

The Matric Science Group program offered by Study Zone Academy is a comprehensive and rigorous academic pathway designed for ambitious students aspiring to excel in the fields of science. This program includes Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, providing students with a strong foundation for future scientific studies.

Subjects Offered

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Urdu, Islamiyat/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

About Program

The Matric Computer Science program offered at Study Zone Academy is a dynamic and comprehensive academic pathway carefully crafted for students who have a passion for technology and computing. This program combines core science subjects with computer science to prepare students for the digital age.

Subjects Offered

Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, Mathematics, English, Urdu, Islamiyat/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

About Program

The Matric General Science program provides a balanced education combining science with humanities subjects. This program is ideal for students who want to keep their options open for both science and arts fields in their future academic pursuits.

Subjects Offered

General Science, Mathematics, English, Urdu, Islamiyat/Ethics, Pakistan Studies
